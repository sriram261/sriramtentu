03 / Featured Product
LIVE FLUENTLOOPAI.COM

Meet FluentLoop.

A browser-based speech practice platform built for students who stutter, born from years of VR speech therapy research at Oklahoma State University’s Stuttering Research Lab.

FluentLoop turns clinical methods into something a student can use anywhere: open the browser, pick a level, record, and get AI-powered feedback in seconds. No headset, no installs.

  • Whisper-powered AI scoring
  • Level-based progression
  • Animated real-time feedback
  • Privacy-first browser stack
  • Built on Next.js + FastAPI
  • Research-backed methodology
